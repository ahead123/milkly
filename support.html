Support

Need help with Milkly? We are here for you.

Contact support

Email us at milklyapp@gmail.com. Please include the details below so we can help quickly.

What to include

  • Your name.
  • The email you use in the Milkly app.
  • A description of the request or issue, including any steps to reproduce it.
  • Supporting documents such as screenshots, screen recordings, or relevant files.

We typically respond within 1-2 business days.
