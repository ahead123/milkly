Privacy Policy

Milkly is a shared baby tracking app. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

What information we collect

  • Account info (e.g., email, name) to let you sign in.
  • Baby tracking data you enter (e.g., feedings, diapers, timestamps, notes).
  • App diagnostics (optional) like crash logs to improve reliability.

How we use information

  • Provide core functionality (saving and syncing your logs).
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse.
  • Improve performance and fix bugs.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only with service providers that help operate the app (e.g., hosting/auth), and only as needed to provide the service.

Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion by contacting us at support@your-email.com (replace this with your real support email).

Your choices

  • Update your account information in the app (if available).
  • Request account/data deletion via the support email above.

Contact

If you have questions, contact us at support@your-email.com.
